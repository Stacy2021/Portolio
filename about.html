About Me

I am Stacy Kibarak, a graduate in Electronics and Computer Engineering and an IEEE member.

I specialize in embedded systems, IoT, sustainable tech innovation, and electronics maintenance.

My work experience spans leading companies such as Engineering for Change, Coca-Cola Beverages Africa, Kenya Breweries Limited, and Gearbox.

Beyond technical work, I am passionate about community, serving as a peer mentor and a leader in tech education and outreach programs.
